Conclusion¶

  • Overall performance between 60-80
  • Females are better than males in overall performance.
  • Females are better than males in reading and writing but males are better in math.
  • Group E is the best group between students.
  • Students had master's degrees are better than others.
  • Students that completed the test preparation course are better than others.
  • Males who had standard lunch are better than others, but females who had free/reduced lunch are better than others.
